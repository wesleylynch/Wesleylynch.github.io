The Process

What Is Paint Correction?

01
Cut Polish — Step One
We use a cutting compound and machine polisher to remove swirl marks, light scratches, water spots, and oxidation from your clear coat. This is the heavy lifting — restoring depth and clarity to the paint.
02
Finishing Polish — Step Two
A finer finishing polish removes any haze left from step one, bringing out a deep, glossy shine and smoothing the surface to perfection.
03
Protective Wax Coat
We finish by applying a hand-applied wax coat to seal and protect the corrected paint, adding shine and a layer of protection against the elements.
